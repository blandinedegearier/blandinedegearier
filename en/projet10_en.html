Odorico

Project: Shooting in the heart of Rennes, looking for mosaic remains made by the Odorico family.

Description: Larges viewscapes shows the evolution of mosaic throughout time, modernity and cities’s growth. The silver photography medium reveal the granual visual and brings nostalgy. The aim of those shooting is to restore and to highlight the heritage. Compositions emphasize details, the diversity and the irregularity of shapes.
